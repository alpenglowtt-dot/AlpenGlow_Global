Overview

Nepal compresses the Himalaya's scale into a trip that doesn't require technical trekking experience — Kathmandu's temple-dense old town, Pokhara's lakeside calm with the Annapurna range as backdrop, and an early-morning mountain flight that gets you eye-level with Everest without the multi-week trek.

Suited to travelers who want genuine Himalayan scenery and culture on a comfortable, non-trekking itinerary.

Itinerary

Day 1-2

Kathmandu

Explore Kathmandu Durbar Square, the Boudhanath Stupa, and Swayambhunath (the Monkey Temple) overlooking the valley.

Day 3-5

Pokhara

Drive or fly to Pokhara — sunrise over the Annapurna range from Sarangkot, a boat ride on Phewa Lake, and a visit to the World Peace Pagoda.

Day 6

Mountain Flight

An early-morning scenic mountain flight along the Himalayan range, weather permitting, with close-up views of Everest and Annapurna.

Day 7

Departure

Transfer to Kathmandu for your international departure.

What's Included

6 nights accommodation Sarangkot sunrise viewpoint Phewa Lake boat ride Himalayan mountain flight (optional) Daily breakfast
