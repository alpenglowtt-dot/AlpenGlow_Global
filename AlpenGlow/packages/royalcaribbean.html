Overview

Southeast Asia by large cruise liner means waking up in Penang, Phuket, or Langkawi without the logistics of separate flights and hotels between each — and a ship with more onboard activity than most resorts. This route departs from Singapore and threads through some of the region's best port stops.

Suited to families and groups who want variety in destinations with the convenience and onboard entertainment of a major cruise line.

Itinerary

Day 1

Embarkation — Singapore

Board in Singapore in the afternoon, with an evening sail-away from Marina Bay Cruise Centre.

Day 2

Day at Sea

A full day onboard to explore the ship's pools, rock climbing wall, and entertainment shows.

Day 3-4

Penang, Malaysia

Dock in Penang for George Town's UNESCO-listed street art and colonial architecture, with free time for the hawker food scene.

Day 5

Phuket, Thailand

A day in Phuket — optional excursions to Phang Nga Bay or simply time on Patong Beach.

Day 6

Day at Sea

A relaxed sea day before returning to Singapore.

Day 7

Disembarkation

Return to Singapore and disembark for your onward journey.

What's Included

6 nights cabin accommodation All onboard meals included Port stops in Penang & Phuket Onboard entertainment & activities Port taxes included
