Overview

The Maldives does one thing better than anywhere on earth: total isolation on your own private sandbank, with an overwater villa and a lagoon so clear you can watch reef fish from your bed. This is a short, deliberately unhurried trip with almost nothing on the itinerary except the resort itself.

Built for honeymooners and anyone who wants a complete digital and decision-making detox.

Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival

Speedboat or seaplane transfer from Male to your overwater villa resort, with the rest of the day free to settle in.

Day 2-3

Resort Days

Snorkelling over the house reef, a sunset dolphin cruise, and an optional sandbank picnic on a deserted sandbar nearby.

Day 4

Spa & Free Day

A couples spa treatment and a free day to swim, paddleboard, or simply do nothing at all.

Day 5

Departure

Speedboat or seaplane transfer back to Male for your international departure.

What's Included

4 nights overwater villa Seaplane or speedboat transfers Sunset dolphin cruise Daily breakfast & dinner Snorkelling gear included
